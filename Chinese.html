Beef and Broccoli Ingredients

  • MAIN INGREDIENTS
  • Beef
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Green Onion
  • MARINADE INGREDIENTS
  • Water
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Baking Soda
  • Light Soy sauce
  • Cornstarch
  • White Pepper
  • Sugar
  • Cooking Wine
  • SAYCE INGREDIENTS
  • Oyster sauce
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Light Soy Sauce
  • Water
  • Cooking wine
  • EXTRA INGREDIENTS
  • Sesame Oil
  • Salt

Procedure

  1. Wash Beef and cut into bite size pieces. Then create beef marinade. Put the beef in the marinade
  2. Cut broccoli, carrots, ginger, garlic, green onion
  3. Make sauce. Blanch the broccoli or a few minutes and take it out. Cook beef for a few minutes then add in veggies and stir-fry
  4. Add sauce and stir-fry again AND YOUR'RE DONE!

Congee Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Water
  • Tomato
  • Radish
  • Chicken or Mushroom powder
  • White or black pepper
  • Century egg
  • Green Onion (optional
  • Soy sauce (To Taste)
  • Fish Sauce (To Taste)

Procedure

  1. Clean rice and soak in cold water after
  2. Transfer rice into pressure coker and add water, chicken powder, and whte pepper. Cook on high pressure for 20 minutes
  3. Once pressure valve goes up, heat in lowest settings and let it rest for a few minutes before releasing pressure
  4. Dice century egg and green onions and put it for garnish. Add fish sauce or Soy sauce to your taste. AND YOU'RE DONE!
