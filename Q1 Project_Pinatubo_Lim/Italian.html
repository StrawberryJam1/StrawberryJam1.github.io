Bruschetta Ingredients

  • Bread (Italian and Sour dough is more preferablle)
  • Tomato
  • Mozarella
  • Basil
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Garlic
  • Salt (Optional)

Procedure

  1. Toast your bread until crunchy (Or has browning but not burnt)
  2. Cut your tomato into thin slices or small cubes and add extra virgin olive oil and chopped garlic
  3. Get your bread rub with garlic and drizzle it with extra virgin olive oil. Then put the tomatoes in top
  4. Add your mozarella on top of the tomatoes and add the basil for garnish and more flavor. THEN YOU"RE DONE

Carbonara Ingredients

  • Pasta noodles
  • egg
  • Pecorino Romano/Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Guanciale/Pancetta/Bacon
  • (NOTE: 1 egg = 100g of pasta)

Procedure

  1. Crack 3 eggs in a bowl and 1 egg yolk and whisk until homogenous.
  2. Shred your Pecorino Romano and add it to the eggs them whisk until homogenous. (No salt if Pecorino is only used)
  3. Boil some pasta and cut up your Guanciale. Put your Guanciale on your cold pan and cooked it on low heat (No need for oil. Oil will be fat of meat)
  4. Turn the heat of the pan and add your pasta directly to the pan after it's done cooking and mix until pasta is coated with oil
  5. Add a ladle of pasta water to the egg & cheese mixture and mis until homogenous.
  6. Add egg & cheese mixture to the pan and mix constantly for it to creamy and not scrambled eggs. AND YOU"RE DONE!
