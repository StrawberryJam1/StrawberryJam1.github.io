Bokkeumbap Ingredients

  • Soy Sauce
  • Minced Pork
  • Sesame Oil
  • Korean Chili Powder (Gochugaru)
  • Kimchi
  • Garlic
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Rice
  • Oil
  • Egg (optional)
  • Green Onion (optional)

Procedure

  1. Combine the pork, soy sauce, sesame oil and chili powder in a bowl and mix well.
  2. Heat the oil in a wok or pan over high heat until hot. Add the seasoned pork and stir-fry for a few minuts until meat begins to brown.
  3. Reduce the heat to medium, add the kimchi, garlic, onion, carrots and stir-fry for a few mintues.
  4. Add the rice to the wok or pan and continue to stir-fry for a few minutes until heated throughand well blended. AND YOU'RE DONE!

Bibimbap Ingredients

  • Oil
  • Minced Beef
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy Sauce
  • Spinach
  • Carrots
  • Cucumbers(Japanese Cucumbers are much preferable)
  • Shiitake Mushrooms
  • Bean sprouts
  • Eggs
  • Korean Hot Bean Paste (Gochujang)(Optinal)
  • Salt
  • Rice

Procedure

  1. Heat the oil in a wok or a pan over medium heat. Add the minced beef, garlic, ginger and soy sauuce, and stir-fry for a few minutes until beef is browned. Turn heat off and set it aside after.
  2. Reheat oil in the wok or pan and stir-fry the carrots, cucumber and mushrooms with salt until tender for a few mintues. Turn off heat and set ingredients aside after.
  3. Blanch the bean sprouts first then the spincah. Sqeeze the spinach into a ball to remove excess water (Don't sqeeze to hard)
  4. Reheat oil in wok or pan and fry an egg
  5. Put the rice on a big bowl and place the egg in the middle, surrounding it would be equal amounts of beef and veggies.
  6. Mix the rice, meat, veggies before eating. AND YOU'RE DONE!
